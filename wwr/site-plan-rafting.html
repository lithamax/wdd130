Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my Rafting website is to market my business and excite potential customers through visually captivating pictures, videos and graphics.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our target customers are people and business who have a keen interest in experiencing the outdoors. Ages range from 12 and above, so we can accomodate businesses and schools. It is also for families who want to take their families for a fun experience outdoors. My audience will want a clear idea of the rafting activities that we offer, the length of time the activities will be and their prices, the location and a description and visuals of the waters we will be rafting at. My audience will usually access my site via mobile device as our marketing campaigns will be the most visible on social media.
